About Us
Over the decades, the rise in population has led to the rise in the demand for more vehicles each passing day. And as the consumers ride along in their vehicle, it has increased the pollution as well. This brought in the need for electric vehicles. Now, these electric vehicles are not only affordable for our pockets but also sustainable to the environment in the long run. With ever-changing technology, the world has evolved accordingly. Energy and transport are interconnected. Soon enough, electric vehicles would become the new normal
Our Goals
Learning about the drawbacks and lack of proper infrastructure for electric vehicles, our CEO Aditya Mishra became determined to make the world a better place to live in, even if it was by the effort of making a business plan that will be beneficial overall. EV Solar works on Solar energy. We as an EV charging station company have a goal to build infrastructure for the need for electric vehicles at different places across India. The key point of the plan to work is Solar energy. Unlike fossil fuels, that is the energy resource that we will not get enough of. We envision to build these fast-charging stations by the first quarter of 2023.
